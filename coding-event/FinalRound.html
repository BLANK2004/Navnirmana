Question 1

Jane, who has a passion for discovering exquisite and meticulously crafted jewelry online. Jane appreciate when a website captivates their attention right from the outset with mesmerizing visuals. Upon landing on an e-commerce platform dedicated to handmade jewelry, they are greeted with stunning photographs of each product set against elegant backgrounds. These visuals instantly enthrall them, igniting a desire to delve deeper into the offerings.

However, visual appeal is just one aspect. This individual values effortless navigation. Travis envision a website layout that is clean, well-organized, and intuitive to navigate. A user-friendly menu system allows them to seamlessly explore various categories such as necklaces, earrings, or bracelets, enhancing their shopping experience with convenience.

Transparency is another critical factor for Travis. They seek clear and comprehensive product descriptions, including details about materials, dimensions, and care instructions. High-resolution images with zoom functionality enable them to examine intricate details, fostering trust and confidence in their purchasing decisions.
